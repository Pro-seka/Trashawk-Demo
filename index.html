About Trashawk

Our Mission

Trashawk is an AI-powered campus accountability system designed to promote cleanliness and environmental responsibility among students. By combining advanced computer vision with a fair penalty and reward system, we're transforming how campuses maintain their environments.

Our system automatically detects litter, identifies responsible individuals, applies appropriate penalties, and rewards those who contribute to campus cleanliness.
